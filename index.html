chiara

the family friend who happens to know medicine

an iphone app for people living with chronic conditions

hey — i'm stefano. if you're here, we probably talked on reddit.

i built chiara because every tracking app i tried made me do the work — and the work is impossible. you can't log your way through a flare, and the real pattern is usually hiding across weeks of sleep, stress and food anyway, with a 24–48h delay no food diary survives.

so i built something that watches passively through your phone's health data and remembers everything. the bad weeks still count, even when logging is the last thing you're capable of.

the founding 15

i'm not looking for users yet. i'm looking for 15 people who live with this every day and will tell me exactly what's broken.

you get a numbered spot, a direct line to me, and every time your feedback changes the product you'll hear it from me personally — "you said x, so i changed y." being nice is not pulling your weight.

what chiara is not: a coach, a chatbot, a tracker with streaks.

she doesn't treat anything and will never promise your symptoms improve. she pays attention so you don't have to do it alone.

your data stays on your phone. i'm not a hospital. i'm not an insurer. that's the whole point.

and tell me one pattern you've always suspected but never been able to prove.
